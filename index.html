Description

Lead massive armies in large-scale battles in this real-time tactics roguelite. Build your army, discover powerful relic synergies, and choose your path through a realm that grows more dangerous with every victory.

Raze The Realm blends the deliberate, formation-driven combat of classic real-time tactics with the run-based progression of modern roguelites. Deploy regiments before each battle, manage formations under pressure, and trigger abilities at the right moment to break enemy morale. Between battles, recruit new units, collect relics, and shape your roster around the strengths of one of three distinct factions.

The campaign unfolds across a non-linear realm map. Every choice — which path to take, which battles to fight, which relics to keep — shifts the difficulty curve of the run ahead. Win, and the realm grows more hostile. Lose, and start a new campaign with the lessons of the last one.

Key features

  • Real-time tactical battles. Command pixel-art regiments in large-scale engagements. Position matters, flanking matters, and morale can decide a fight before the last unit falls.
  • Roguelite campaign. Choose your route through a branching realm map. Each run brings new relics, new enemies, and new ways for things to go very wrong.
  • Multiple unique factions. Each plays radically differently — for example The Plagued (fearless shambling infantry), The Wildhost (moose riders and tree titans), and The Damned (hellfire-summoning cultists), with more to discover.
  • Relic synergies. Stack relics to build wild, run-defining combos that reward bold drafting decisions.
  • Skirmish & custom maps. Jump straight into battles, or build battlefields from image files and share them via Steam Workshop.
  • Pixel art at scale. Hundreds of animated units on screen, hand-crafted art, and battlefields that read at a glance.
